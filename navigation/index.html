Creating navigational links within a website

There are several techniques to navigating within a website. the semantic tag for naviagation is your <nav> tag. You can navigate though your website by using relative adressing. For out examples we will use the unordered list tag. (<ul>) Our ecamples will consist of:

  • page to page within the same folder
  • page to page between folders
  • staying on the current page
  • going to a specific place on the current page (on page navigation)
  • going to a specific place on another page (Choosing a class)

Navigational menus are placed on your <header> semantic area. they may also be placed in your <footer> area.
